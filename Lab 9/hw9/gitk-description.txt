The top left section shows the commits from branches in a tree-like format, information about the branches, and relationship between branches. Below that is the SHA1 ID of the selected commit.
The top middle section shows the name and email of the author who made the commits.
The top right section shows the date and time of the commits.

The bottom left section shows the ChangeLog of the commit, set to ‘Diff’ format.
The bottom right section shows the files that are related to the selected commit.
